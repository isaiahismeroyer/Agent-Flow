FAQ

Questions agents ask before using AI follow-up.

Will the AI sound like my brokerage?

AgentFlow is positioned as a structured follow-up assistant. The setup process should define tone, qualification questions, and handoff rules before launch.

How fast does AgentFlow respond?

The goal is to respond within seconds of a new inquiry so the first message lands while the prospect is still engaged.

What does setup include?

Setup should cover lead sources, qualification fields, appointment flow, contact details, and the preferred handoff process.

Can it work with my CRM?

The current positioning supports CRM handoff planning. Specific CRM connections should be confirmed during the demo based on your stack.

Does this replace me or my ISA?

No. AgentFlow handles speed-to-lead and structured qualification so you or your team can focus on high-value conversations.

Why is there a setup fee?

The setup fee covers configuring the follow-up flow, qualification logic, lead routing, and launch details for your real estate process.
